About the V. J. High School


We believe in providing a rich, stimulating environment for personalized as well as collaborative learning. This environment is conducive to fostering Academic Excellence and CreativeBrilliance. We are challenged at all times with global development and advancement. The schooleducation, being the foundation and the fundamental stage of a child’s development, has to work towards facing this challenge and strengthening a society. The ingeniously designed CBSE curriculum at The VJHS School is in sync with our motto, ‘EDUCATE, EXCEL, EMPOWER’.

Academic Session Resumes with Virtual Classrooms and Online Pedagogy

Never stop learning, because life never stops teaching.

As you all know, due to the CoViD19 pandemic, the world is facing a crisis like never before. This is an unprecedented situation in terms of breadth and scale, from a global health, social and economic standpoint. The VJHS School Team and the management sincerely wishes for the safety and well being of all the children, parents and family members.
We, at The VJHS School, stand on a strong foundation and are committed to Academic Excellence & Creative Brilliance. Our educators, who believe in imparting the basic skill of Lifelong Learning,have been working very hard on learning and preparing themselves for continuing the knowledge sharing process through new mediums using technology. It has been a learning journey for our educators as well, and we hope the children enjoy and adapt to this as long as the lockdown lasts.
Today, every house is a school and every parent is a teacher. We, at The VJHS School, through our Online Teaching, Worksheets and Guidance and through our Virtual Classrooms, aim to bring routine and discipline into the lives of our children. Of course, your support as parents is absolutely imperative to make this a success.
